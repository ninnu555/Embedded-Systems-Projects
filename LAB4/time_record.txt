However, for smaller array dimensions (like 10 in our case), the overhead implied for managing the parallel processing results being more costy than the benefits the parallel architecture gives, making the single-core architecture faster than the parallel one for this specific case.

Moreover, there is a difference in speedup contribute between that provided by the change of architecture and the vectorization enabling, which sees the vectorization version giving a higher contribute than the parallel one.
